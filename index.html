About

I am a first-year Computer Science Ph.D. student in Systems Research Lab at Rutgers - New Brunswick, supervised by Prof. Sudarsun Kannan.

My current research interests lie in Operating System design and its intersection with Storage Systems, Distributed Systems, and Disaggregated Memory/Storage. I am actively working on File System optimization and extension, especially focusing on disaggregated storage system design and corresponding caching/prefetching mechanisms.
